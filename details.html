About The Thrift Shop

The meaning of Thrift shop is a shop or business that sells used goods from outside and within the country. Also known as thrift shop. since 2013, the trade in used goods has started to enter Indonesia, starting from rare goods to goods with well-known brands.
The product that is often sold is clothing. There are also used product such as books, and even electronic furniture that is stiil in good condition. This shop sells for charitable purposes. Usually thrift shoops are opened in order to raise money for charity.
The meaning of the word Thrift in English means saving. According to Cambridge Dictonary, Thrift means using money wisely, especially by avoiding waste
